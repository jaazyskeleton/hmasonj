About Me

Hello, I am H. Mason. J.

I am a writer and storyteller with a passion for crafting compelling narratives that captivate readers.

My work explores the feeling of being human, delving into themes of love, loss, identity, and the complexities of the human mind.

My inspiration behind what I do comes from many different sources, but I have found myself to be under the inspirational influence of “Silas Orion”, the creator of the youtube channel “Doctor Nowhere”.

I believe that the concept of depicting unique creatures that aren’t human with human emotions is beautiful though, my work does encourage others to create these character’s appearance using their imagination.

My love for searching for the meaning of life and the human condition has led me to explore various philosophical and psychological concepts, which often find their way into my writing.

I am also a part artist, and I enjoy creating visual representations of the characters and worlds I create in my writing.

This is only a small factor of who I am but it is a part of my creative process that I find to be very rewarding.

Along side my writing, I am also an experienced programmer who made this very website solo.

I program in JavaScript, Python and HTML. These are my primary programming languages (only the ones I am "good" at).

I would like to thank my family and friends for my the support you have on me, and this also includes all of you viewing this website as it really proves that I'm not wasting my time. Thank you - H. Mason. J
